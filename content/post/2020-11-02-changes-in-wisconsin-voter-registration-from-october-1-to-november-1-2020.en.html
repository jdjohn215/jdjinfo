Eagle-eyed readers may notice that the total number of registered voters is not exactly identical between the files. I believe this is because the WECs files are created from a live system continuously receiving updates from local clerks. Consequently, data for individual records may be updated between the time it takes to run, e.g., the county and ward summary scripts.
